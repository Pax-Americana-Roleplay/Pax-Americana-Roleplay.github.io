How to edit the sidebar

As there are two columns under the sidebar already, you should be able to add or update any lines you need to accordingly. If something on the right sidebar column goes to two lines, use "shift+enter" under the left sidebar column for each line it goes over to keep everything even.

If you want to recreate the sidebar from scratch, but aren't sure how to get the sub columns back in, edit the page below to however you want to format your sidebar. The name of the page should be whatever you want the topmost line on the sidebar to read.

Once you've edited the page accordingly, click the gear icon on the right side of the "Sidebar" template to edit said template, then drag the page above into the template.

Once the page is in the template, click the three dots next to the page and choose "turn into > header 3". Delete the instructions line found in the template block. Everything should now be properly formatted into the template block.

Drag the template to the second column and click the button to add your new sidebar to your "Wikipedia" template!
